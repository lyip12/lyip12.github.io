All Time Statistics

The FIFA World Cup™ is the biggest single-event sporting competition in the world and is played by the senior men’s national teams from the 209 Member Associations of FIFA. The competition has been played every four years since the inaugural tournament in 1930, except in 1942 and 1946 when it was not held because of the Second World War. The 20 FIFA World Cup tournaments have been won by eight different national teams.
